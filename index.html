Signs that deserve faster attention

A strong online doctor experience should also explain when symptoms may need urgent review instead of routine home care.

  • Shortness of breath or chest pain
  • High fever that keeps rising
  • Confusion, fainting, or severe weakness
  • Rash with swelling or signs of infection
  • Persistent vomiting or dehydration concerns
